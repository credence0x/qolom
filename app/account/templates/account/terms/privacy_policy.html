INTRODUCTION

Qolom offers an online queueing platform that makes it easy for service providers and the general public to create and join lines virtually. Qolom is dedicated to serving you and ensuring the security of your information, to enable a hitch free experience. This Privacy Policy describes the information we process to support our services and is intended to set certain standards across our operations, to enable the achievement of the aforementioned objectives.

OVERVIEW

The objective of this Privacy Policy is to explain how we collect, use, disclose and protect your information, including any personal information. This privacy policy applies to the services provided by Qolom (“we” or “us”).
This policy applies to information we collect when you use our website (and mobile application) (collectively, “Services”) or otherwise interact with us as described below. We aim to handle personal data provided to us in compliance with applicable data privacy and protection laws.
We reserve the right to change, update, modify, amend, adjust and/or add to or subtract from the content of this privacy policy. Notification of such change will be communicated to you primarily by a revision of the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document, and in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as by adding a statement to the homepages of our website or by sending you an email notification).
We advice you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you interact with us, or periodically so as to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.
Use of our website implies consent to the content of this privacy policy.
In the event that further/special permission is required from users, we shall further solicit for consent.

THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal and non personal information.

Personal information is information that uniquely identifies a user. To gain full access to our website and services, you must create a Qolom account. When you create an account, we collect personal information (e.g. email address, username, country, etc) which you provide to us. Due to the nature of the services we provide, personal information is needed to serve unique content to each user.

We also collect non-personal information such as anonymous usage data, referring or exit pages, browser type, IP addresses and data generated based on submitted data or number of clicks. To avoid ambiguity, we will explain some of the data we collect in some detail:

1. Log Data: Log data is anonymous and automatic data collected by our web server about the time a user visits the website, when the website page was requested, the referring or exit page, device information including the hardware model, mobile network information, operating system and version, browser type, the IP address requesting the page, amongst others.

2. Cookies: Cookies are small text files which can uniquely or anonymous identify users. Considering the purpose of the website, some of the cookies are essential to ensure that the website functions properly as almost every page has to uniquely identify users to show information relevant to the specific user. Therefore a cookie is sent containing a unique random reference to identify you.
We also use cookies to prevent malicious activities from attackers. All cookies that we use presently are essential to ensuring optimum consumer satisfaction. As the website develops, we may allow for users to disable non-essential cookies.
We never store sensitive information in cookies.

3. Location Data: Upon getting consent from a user's browser, we may also collect information relating to the location of a user for the sole purpose of improving search results for the particular user.

4. Profile Data: We also store information such as your profile picture, username, etc provided to us during registration and profile updates.

HOW WE SECURE YOUR INFORMATION

We implement security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access. Your account is protected primarily by your account password and we implore that you take steps to keep your personal information secure by not disclosing your password to any individual, group, or third party; and by logging out of your account after each use, to prevent your account from being compromised.
We further protect your information from potential security breaches by implementing certain technological security measures including encryption, firewalls and secure socket layer technology.
We limit access to your personal information to employees that have a necessary and integral business reason to know such information (such as database managers and customer support). This is to help us perform essential tasks such as providing consumer assistance, data security or other administrative purposes.
Qolom takes reasonable measures to help protect all information about you from loss, unlawful disclosure, alteration, misuse and unauthorized access, and destruction.
Additionally, Qolom has put in place policies, security measures and safety mechanism with the central objective of protecting the confidentiality and security of your non public personal information, and prohibiting unlawful disclosure of such information in accordance with said policies.
However, these measures do not guarantee that your information will not be accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed by an unforeseen breach of said security measures. By using our Service, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to assume these risks.
